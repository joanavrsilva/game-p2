How to play
the Rock Paper Scissors Game
Level 1 

    
      This level corresponds to the traditional rock paper scissors game.
      In the game level, start by choosing one of the following hands:
      
      After your choice, the computer will choose a hand.
       
      You win:
      If you choose the paper hand and the computer choose the rock hand;
      
      If you choose the scissors hand and the computer choose the paper hand;
      
      If you choose the rock hand and the computer choose the scissors hand;
      
    
      You lose:
      You lose in the inverse situation:
      
    
      Tie:
      It's a tie if you choose the same hand as the computer.
