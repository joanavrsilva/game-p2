How to play
the Rock Paper Scissors Game
Level 2 

      This level corresponds to the traditional rock paper scissors game.
      Start by choosing one of the following hands:
      
      After your choice, the computer will choose a hand.    
      You win:
      If you choose the paper hand and the computer choose the rock hand or the spock hand
      
      If you choose the scissors hand and the computer choose the paper hand or the lizard hand
      
      If you choose the rock hand and the computer choose the scissors hand or the lizard hand
      
      If you choose the spock hand and the computer choose the rock hand or the scissors hand
      
      If you choose the lizard hand and the computer choose the paper hand or the spock hand
      

      You lose:
      You lose in the inverse situation
      
    
      Tie:
      It's a tie if you choose the same hand as the computer
